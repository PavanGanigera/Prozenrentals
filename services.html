SYNCHRONIZING PANELS, AUTOMATIC TRANSFER SWITCHES & DISTRIBUTION BOARD

Synchronizing Panels are specifically designed to manage and control multiple generators, ensuring stable and efficient power supply. These panels support both manual and automatic synchronizing operations, making them ideal for complex power systems where reliability and coordination between generators are essential. They are commonly used across industries to streamline power distribution and enhance operational flexibility.

Our Automatic and Manual Transfer Switches (ATS/MTS) are engineered to provide seamless power transitions during utility outages. The ATS is connected to both the utility and the generator, ensuring immediate switch-over to backup power when needed, guaranteeing uninterrupted power flow in critical scenarios.
