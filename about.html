About Progen Power Rentals

Progen Power Rentals is one of Saudi Arabia’s most trusted and leading names in generator and aerial work platform (AWP) rentals, proudly headquartered in Al Jubail. We specialize in delivering advanced, high-performance equipment designed to support your operations safely, reliably, and efficiently. We offer a wide range of diesel generators, aerial work platforms, boom lifts (manlifts), and scissor lifts—ideal for construction, industrial, and infrastructure projects. Our rental fleet includes both canopied and containerized Cummins and Perkins generators, as well as specialty lifting equipment like crawler boom lifts and spider lifts, meeting all your power and height-access requirements.

At Progen, we are proud to power projects with equipment built by world-leading manufacturers such as Cummins, Perkins, JLG, and Genie. Our emergency power solutions are self-contained, sound-attenuated, and fully mobile—ready to support your temporary or emergency power needs across commercial, industrial, and institutional sectors. Our AWP fleet (ranging from 9.8M to 40M) offers unmatched safety and functionality. Equipped with advanced features like anti-entrapment technology, load sensing systems, automatic traction control, auto-braking, motion & tilt alarms, and self-leveling baskets, our machines ensure secure operations at height. With excess capacity baskets and non-marking solid tires for indoor and outdoor use, our lifts are built for productivity, safety, and versatility.

We are more than just a rental provider—we’re a team of committed professionals delivering 24/7 customer support and personalized service. Backed by years of industry expertise and a growing blue-chip client base across Saudi Arabia, Progen Power Rentals continues to set new standards in equipment rental and customer satisfaction.

Why Choose Progen Power Rentals?

Progen Power Rentals leads the industry with top-tier generator and equipment rental solutions tailored to diverse needs. We specialize in Diesel Generators, Load Banks, AWP Lifts, Air Compressors, Material Handling Equipment, and more — all at highly competitive rates. Whether you require equipment for a day, a week, or longer, we provide flexible rental options to suit your schedule and budget.

Our diesel generators, ranging from 30 KVA to 1250 KVA, guarantee uninterrupted power supply for various industries, including oil & gas, construction, manufacturing, events, and textiles. From residential projects to large-scale industrial operations in Riyadh and beyond, Progen Power Rentals is your trusted partner for reliable, efficient, and cost-effective power solutions.
