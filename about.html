Who We Are?

Progen Power is a leading provider of temporary power solutions, heavy equipment rental, manpower supply, and transportation services. Built on technical expertise, reliability, and a strong service culture, we support projects of every scale — from urban industries to remote and challenging locations.

We aim to be your complete project partner, delivering safe, cost-effective, and on-time solutions.

Why Choose Us

Progen Power is a trusted provider of temporary and long-term power rental solutions, built on a foundation of technical expertise, reliability, and a strong commitment to service excellence. Since our establishment, we have been delivering dependable power systems to clients operating in challenging environments, critical operations, and remote project locations.

With a versatile fleet of diesel generators ranging from 20 KVA to 2000 KVA, we support industries across construction, oil & gas, manufacturing, utilities, events, and infrastructure development. Our success is driven by high-quality equipment, timely response, and a skilled technical team ensuring smooth performance from installation to decommissioning.
