POWER GENERATOR RENTALS (365KVA – 440KVA)

Advanced Technology & Superior Performance

Our 365 KVA to 440 KVA diesel generators feature canopied enclosures, robust engines, and globally recognized alternators to deliver powerful and consistent performance. They incorporate cutting-edge in-cylinder combustion technology, meeting the latest emission standards without relying on additional after-treatment systems. With outstanding transient load response and the ability to handle 100% block loading, these gensets are built for reliability under pressure. The sleek design, premium finish, and compact form factor ensure an excellent power-to-weight ratio and easy on-site installation.

Eco-Conscious Power Delivery

Built with industry-defining engine technology, our generators comply with the revised MoEF emission regulations, ensuring clean and sustainable energy output. The 400 KVA generator models are engineered for minimal noise emissions, making them ideal for use in sound-sensitive environments.

Low Operating Cost

Our generator sets are designed for maximum fuel efficiency, even at partial loads, resulting in significantly reduced operational costs. The high-quality build ensures durability and dependability, making them a cost-effective choice for continuous or backup power needs.

Complete Power from One Source

Versatile Equipment Fleet

We offer a diverse range of silent diesel generators from 365 KVA to 440 KVA, supported by essential accessories like load banks, fuel tanks, power cables, HV/LV panels, and ATS systems. Our flexible rental solutions are designed to accommodate any site requirement, ensuring your power demands are met efficiently and reliably.
