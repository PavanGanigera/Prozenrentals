600KVA TO 800 KVA DIESEL GENERATOR SET

Latest Technology and Unmatched Performance

The 600 KVA to 800 KVA diesel generator sets are built with a robust engine and a premium-grade alternator to deliver exceptional performance. Utilizing advanced in-cylinder combustion technology, these generators comply with the latest emission regulations without needing any external after-treatment system. They respond rapidly to power demands, even handling 100% block load with ease. Their smart design offers a refined finish and compact build, providing an optimal balance of power and size.

Environment-Friendly Power

Equipped with next-generation engines, these generator sets meet the updated MoEF environmental norms, ensuring reduced emissions and a cleaner output. They deliver reliable energy while maintaining the lowest noise levels in their class, making them an eco-conscious choice for both indoor and outdoor operations.

Lowest Operating Cost

Designed for longevity and high efficiency, these generators are engineered to perform with minimal operational costs. Every component is optimized to work harmoniously, ensuring fuel savings and stable performance, even when operating under partial load conditions.

Single Source Power Assurance

Our expansive support network is always on standby to deliver technical service and spare parts whenever needed. This extensive reach ensures uninterrupted performance and total peace of mind, giving you the confidence of dependable power from a single, trusted source.

Fleet of Equipment

We offer an extensive selection of power solutions for rent, including silent diesel generators ranging from 30 KVA to 2000 KVA. Our inventory also includes supporting accessories like load banks, fuel tanks, power cables, HV/LV panels, and ATS systems to cater to a wide variety of energy demands.

Engine

These generator sets feature a 12-cylinder, V-type, 4-stroke engine with radiator cooling for superior thermal performance. The square engine layout enhances reliability and stability. A sophisticated air intake system with external aftercooling and dual pump-loop architecture ensures consistent performance at higher altitudes. Enhanced fuel economy is achieved through the PT fuel system equipped with ESTC injectors, enabling precise engine speed control under load. The dual fuel filtration system—with a pre-filter, water separator, and Water-In-Fuel sensor—ensures clean fuel delivery and long-term engine protection.
